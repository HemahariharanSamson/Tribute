The Heart of My Home 💞

They are the ultimate definition of cool, quirky, and cuddly, and they prove that love and laughter are the keys to a happy life.

So let's raise a paw (or a glass 🥂) to the love story that just keeps getting better. I want to celebrate the Love and Laughter of my parents, who have made an indelible impact on my life and on the lives of those around them. They are the heart of our home, and their love and laughter will forever be cherished in my heart.
